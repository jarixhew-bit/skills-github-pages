🏙 大阪 · Conrad 40楼Osaka · Conrad 40F

C:GRILL 海鲜烧烤C:GRILL ⭐4.4

40楼薪火炭烤，主打全球直送海鲜，200米高空夜景。菜单也有牛排单品，点餐时选海鲜/鸡肉款即可避开。窗边位建议提前指定40F wood-fire grill, globally-sourced seafood, 200m night view. Steak is on the menu too — order seafood/chicken to avoid beef. Request a window table in advance
⚠ 周一二休 · 建议7/23或7/24晚⚠ Closed Mon/Tue · suggest evening of Jul 23 or 24

蔵 KURA 铁板烧＆寿司KURA ⭐4.6

同层铁板烧+寿司柜台，主厨现场表演。套餐固定含一道牛肉主菜（国产牛/黑毛和牛可选升级），非自由替换鸡肉/海鲜——订位时问清是否能整段换成海鲜或鸡肉；握寿司、前菜、汤品不受影响Teppanyaki + sushi counter on the same floor, chef performs tableside. The set course has a fixed beef main (domestic beef, upgradable to wagyu) — it is not a free swap to chicken/seafood; ask at booking whether the whole course can be substituted. Sushi, starters and soup are unaffected
⚠ 周三休 · 建议7/23或7/24晚⚠ Closed Wed · suggest evening of Jul 23 or 24

一兰拉面 道顿堀南馆ICHIRAN Dotonbori South ⭐4.3

全球知名连锁拉面，24小时营业，可自订浓淡/软硬/辣度。招牌一人食隔间座位是特色体验；也有多人桌台座（非24小时，较早打烊）。纯猪骨汤底拉面，不含牛肉，但风格随性简单，比其他几家正式许多，适合想吃点熟悉的宵夜口味时World-famous ramen chain, open 24 hours, fully customizable broth/noodle firmness/spice. Signature solo dining booths are a novelty; table seating for groups also available (closes earlier than the 24h booth floor). Pork-bone broth, no beef, but very casual — a different register from the other picks, good if you want something familiar and low-key
✅ 24小时营业（桌台座较早打烊）✅ Open 24h (table floor closes earlier)

🏙 东京 · 四季大手町 39楼Tokyo · Four Seasons Otemachi 39F

est 米其林一星法餐est (1 Michelin Star) ⭐4.3

米其林一星法餐，95%食材来自日本本地农渔，套餐式上菜。食客评价确认餐厅有和牛courses，但也证实厨房会认真处理"不吃某类食材"的要求（曾配合过不吃猪肉的客人），订位时说明不吃牛肉即可1-Michelin-star French, 95% Japan-sourced ingredients, set-menu format. Reviews confirm a wagyu course exists, but also confirm the kitchen genuinely accommodates dietary exclusions (one review noted a no-pork request handled well) — just flag no-beef at booking
⚠ 周三休 · 建议7/26/27/28晚，务必提前订位⚠ Closed Wed · suggest Jul 26/27/28 evening, book well ahead

PIGNETO 意大利餐厅PIGNETO ⭐4.4

39楼露台，可望皇居+富士山（天气好时），全天营业无休无午休。披萨、意面、烤物为主，评价中主菜有牛小排选项，可点披萨/海鲜意面/烤鸡代替，氛围比 est 更轻松39F terrace overlooking the Imperial Palace + Mt Fuji (clear days), open daily with no lunch gap. Pizza, pasta, oven-roasted mains — short rib is one reviewed option, order pizza/seafood pasta/roast chicken instead; more relaxed than est
✅ 全天营业，无休无午休✅ Open daily, no gap
